CloneTTS:Universal Text-to-Speech Models are Zero-Shot Learners

Abstract

Recent attention has been drawn to TTS models based on audio codec and LLM (large language model). However, they commonly face challenges such as the exponential increase in sequence length due to multi-layer codebooks during audio encoding, insufficient information from single-layer codebooks, and the inconsistency in timbre between acoustic tokens generated by LLM in autoregressive mode. To address these issues, we propose a method called CloneTTS. Experimental results indicate that, compared to several strong baseline models, CloneTTS demonstrates superior performance.
